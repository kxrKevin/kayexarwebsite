COMING SOON???

KAYEXAR (Prounounced "KXR") is an electronic music duo formed in 2020 by CAF Veteran Roberto Kuehr and Kevin Li. The two were introduced through mutual friends at Western University and quickly discovered their shared passion for music.

After the initial success of their 2021 and 2022 releases, which garnered hundreds of thousands of streams, the duo entered 2023 with high expectations. Tracks like "On My Own," "Want It," and "Caffeine" helped solidify Kayexar's presence in the music industry, surpassing a million streams. These tracks were also featured on radio stations and charted in top positions in various cities.

As they move into 2024, Kayexar continues to explore new creative opportunities and musical styles. With releases like "Never Look Back" and their latest summer hit, "Sorry," the duo blends various house EDM styles with unique, self-taught production techniques. The future looks incredibly promising for Kayexar as they continue to push boundaries and evolve their sound.

Heading into 2025, Kayexar is excited to take their music to new heights. With a growing fanbase and an ever-expanding presence in the industry, they are preparing for more releases and live performances that will further define their sound and impact on the electronic music scene.
